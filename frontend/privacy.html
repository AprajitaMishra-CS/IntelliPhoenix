Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At IntelliPhoenix, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.


* Your privacy matters. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring secure healthcare interactions.


Information Collection

We collect information from you when you visit our site, register on our site, place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, or interact with us in other ways.

We may collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Password (stored securely in encrypted form)
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Appointment Details
  • Symptoms entered for AI analysis

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect from you in the following ways:

  • To personalize your experience on our site.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To generate AI-based symptoms suggestions.
  • Maintain security and prevent unauthorized access.
  • To respond to your inquiries and support needs.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.
  • To manage your appointments.
  • To analyze symptoms entered for AI analysis.

The AI symptom analyzer provides preliminary suggestions based on the information you provide. However, it is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

It is not a replacement for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.

Data Security

We use appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. This includes encryption, firewalls, and secure server hosting.

Passwords are stored securely using strong hashing algorithms, and access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

We may also release information when it's release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property or safety.

Your information may only be shared with authorized healthcare professionals involved in your care.

Our website may use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences.
  • Improve user experience.
  • Analyze how our website is used.
